# TBBC is coming back **By Carl Fisherson**
Senior Journalist, TBBC News ![The new BBC News studio in Broadcasting House](https://www.newscaststudio.com/wp-content/gallery/bbc-news-studio-b-2022/BBC-News_studio-b_set-design_01.JPEG) It is true. After a year of absence, TBBC is soft-launching as a news website. We are looking out to provide quality news for the average reader, with factual reports, clear opinions and a wide coverage on developer affairs, as well as culture, sports, weather, the future and the worklife here in the MU. After days of working on our new website, we present you a revamped look, with new fonts, a redesigned, more modern logo and a cohesive look. The website is subject to change, and we look forward to hear your feedback! Starting today, we will now cover not only the Developer Sanctuary, but the whole Mica Union as well as the MEA, as well as a whole new range of subjects, like the future of our generations as well as that of those to come, the cultural life of the Sanctuarians as well as the worklife, the key of our modern development society. Our team of journalists and developers presents you high-quality stories and headlines, curated for you, the average viewer. At the Broadcasting House here in Webhdad, we highlight what matters, and dismiss the unimportant details. Because news are made to inform, not to waste time. The new, streamlined TBBC website presents you a vast number of stories at a glance away, and, together with a modern look and feel, further enhances the TBBC News experience. Starting off from the top, with a beautiful font and an organised experience that gives you links to organisations across our company, to the bottom, where useful shortcuts to our policies and guidance are held; everything was thought from the ground up, to enhance the experience and bring what's important to the front of the page.
