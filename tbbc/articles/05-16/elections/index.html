# 16/5 Developer Sanctuary Elections **TBBC World Service**
16 May 2021 Today's elections are going ahead as planned, with a total of 11 candidates. *These elections follow a ten-day exile of the previous leader, FireCube#4142.* Those competing in the election are: - **Alur#2255**, standing for Alur Liberal Radicals - **TechnoBiscuit#3540**, standing for [The Biscuit Cult](https://dev801.github.io/discord) - **Chancellor Cat107#5775**, standing for Cat Potato Alliance - **Dukemz#7766**, standing for Emeril Alliance - **FireCube#4142**, standing for Cube on Fire - **Legacy Aviator#7010**, standing for Legacy Democratic Liberals - **Mostafa Walid#1316**, an independent candidate. - **Mune#9263 (Colin Kiama)**, standing for Sparkland Movement - **Nextin Hekonray#6621**, standing for Nextin Freedom Movement - **Phantom GhostX#5651**, an independent candidate. The current leader of Developer Sanctuary, Zeealeid#8472, has surprisingly not entered the election.
